An assistive system that takes a
- job description, 
- the candidate’s CV, 
- and an interview transcript, 

and returns a structured alignment report. 

It 
  **maps** JD themes to what appears in the CV and transcript (aligned / partial / missing), 
  **highlights** strong cases to reuse in later rounds and problem spots (weak or vague answers, with quotes where possible), 
  **suggests** how to reframe existing experience for this vacancy, plus light rubric-style feedback on clarity, evidence, and relevance. 

Disclaimer: not legal or hiring advice and not a substitute for human coaching.
